Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

Some accessibility concerns I discovered after completing these challeges is that in challenge 1, the viewer might get confused because there is no option to go to assignment 2 which is the index.html page they are already on. I could address this by including a link to assignment 2 that would just stay on the page they're on. Another accessibility concern for challenge 3, forms, could be that the form doesn't do anything once filled out. I would address this by having an output appear when they fill in the form.
